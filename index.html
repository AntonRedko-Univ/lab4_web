Anteiku Cafe

This cafe is most likely the model of Tokyo Ghoul's Anteiku Cafe (あんていく).
It is conveniently located across the street from Nerima Station's South Exit.
Located on the 2nd floor, this old-fashioned cafe offers great street views.
The coffee is very dense and tasty. The cafe owner spoke little English but was hospitable.

Found us
